Our Story

Founded by Vincent, an entrepreneur and a mariner, MSB Charters was born out of a desire to share the beauty and freedom of the ocean with others who seek to embrace such elegance and grandeur.
Over the years with a focus on luxury, safety and exceptional service, we've curated a fleet of sleek and sophisticated vessels to meet the diverse needs of our clients.
